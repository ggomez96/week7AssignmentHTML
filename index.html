Welcome to the Formula 1 Fan Community Site

Welcome to the F1 Community, where you can learn the gists of the most popular racing. If you're new to the racing world, this is the perfect site to get your info! Formula One is the highest class of racing world wide since 1950. It uses a point based system to determine the winners in two championships.
Depending the circuit, there are 5 types of tyres, consisting of: Hard, Medium, Softs, Intermediates and Wets. Drivers can go from 0 - 60 mph within 3 seconds and up to 230 mph.
